Chenyu Zhao

Master Student, AIoT Lab, Tsinghua University, China.

I am currently a master researcher at the AIoT Lab within the institution of Data and Information, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Xinlei Chen. Prior to that, received the B.E. degree of Electronic Information Science and Technology from Northwest University in China, and the B.S. degree of Electronic System Engineering from University of Essex in UK, in 2022.

My current work focuses on advancing sensing, control, and edge computing within lightweight drone-based systems. I enjoy building proof-of-concepts for innovative applications, encompassing both hardware and software aspects.

Emails: zhaocy22@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn or zcyhi@foxmail.com

Research Interests: Mobile Computing, Cyber-Physical Systems, Internet of Things, Sensing, Embedded AI, Robotics and Quadrotors and Drones. You can find details in my CV.
